# App name ideas

Pick one you like, or mix and match.

- **Launchpad** — One place to launch any app idea. Short, memorable.
- **Forge** — You forge apps from ideas. Strong, builder vibe.
- **Promptly** — Apps from prompts, fast. Playful.
- **Stack** — As in “build your stack.” Simple, dev-friendly.
- **Loom** — You loom apps from threads of ideas. Creative.
- **Spark** — Spark an app in minutes. Energetic.
- **Craft** — Craft apps with AI. Clear and calm.
- **Bolt** — Build in a bolt. Speed + simplicity.
- **Scaffold** — Scaffold web and mobile apps. Nods to devs.
- **Mint** — Mint new apps. Fresh, new.

**Current placeholder in the app:** **Launchpad** (you can change it in one place later).
